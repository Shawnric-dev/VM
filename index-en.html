Biography

Full Name: Venâncio António Bila Mondlane

Profession: Forest Engineer and Bank Clerk

Date of Birth: January 17, 1974

Nationality: Mozambican

Place of Birth: Lichinga – Niassa

Email: venamondlane@gmail.com

PROFESSIONAL CAREER

2002-2025: Senior Technician at Millennium Bim Group, specialized in economic-financial analysis of large companies for credit operations, factoring, Leasing and large investments; Corporate Manager of large companies; Senior Technician for creating banking rules and procedures – Millennium Bim Group.

2002 – 2004: Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry teacher at Zedequias Manganhela and Maxaquene High Schools.

2000 - 2002: Specialized in development and implementation of international ISO standards for the certification of forestry products and other import and export products on a large scale in ports and airports, at the Swiss multinational SGS – Société Générale de Surveillance.

CIVIC-CULTURAL JOURNEY

2025: Largest and more engaged digital platforms among politicians from PALOP (Portuguese-speaking African Countries)

2024: Reached the highest African record for simultaneous views on Facebook Live broadcasts.

2020 - 2021: Invited as the main speaker in international virtual debates with emphasis on:

  • July 2, 2020: Main speaker invited by Central Angola 7311 to address the theme "Local Authorities: Expectations and Priorities".
  • October 17, 2020: Main speaker invited by the Organization "Youth for Local Authorities of Guinea-Bissau" to address the theme "Model of decentralization of power in Mozambique";
  • February 19, 2021: Mozambique’s representative in a debate involving all Portuguese-speaking countries (CPLP) whose theme was "Local Authorities in Lusophony – Processes and Obstacles"

2014 - 2016: Columnist for several national weeklies, notably “Savana” and “Canal de Moçambique”

2003 – 2014: resident commentator on several television and radio programs, notably "Especial Eleições" and “Pontos de Vista” on STV; “21ª Hora Informação" and “A Hora da Verdade” on TIM; “A voz do Povo” and "O Grande Debate” TV Miramar (radio); "Mais Jovem" on TVM; political commentator for RDP Africa in Portugal, TV8 in Angola and more.

2002 – 2013: Columnist for several literary-cultural publications, with emphasis on the largest literary magazine in the country, "Lua Nova" by the Mozambican Writers' Association; the "Universitário” from UEM; "Proler" from the Portuguese Language Bibliographic Fund; the "Cultural" from Jornal Noticias;

2013: The only young man from CPLP selected for the International Leadership Programme (IVLP), an initiative of then US President Barack Obama and coordinated by the US State Department;

2008- 2012: Speaker in several forums, including:

  • Portuguese Cultural Center where he spoke, to a huge audience of University Students, on the theme "The Role of Reading in Human Development";
  • UEM Final Year Students’ Association on the theme "Housing for Young People".

2007: Founding member of the IPG Association – Pro-Governance Initiative;

2000: Coordinator of the “Jovens Solidários” Movement in Kambukwana District to support the victims of the 2000 floods. Having been the subject of exclusive journalistic coverage by TVM.

1990: Creation of Édipus and the Artistic-Literary Workshop at artist Mikas home studio.

POLITICAL CAREER

2024: Candidate in the Presidential elections, having won with 55% according to the parallel count, but the victory was denied by the regime based on an extremely brutal police repression. He then organized the largest demonstration in the country's history, which lasted 4 months.

2023: Elected unanimously and acclaimed Renamo‘s Head List for Maputo City Local Elections, achieving a technically proven result of 55%; Coordinator and Promoter of the largest marches contesting electoral results in Maputo City’s history, over a period of 45 days, with repercussions and media coverage among the largest media channels on the planet.

2020: Deputy of Renamo Party in the Parliament for the IX Legislature, assuming the role of Rapporteur of the Bench;

2019:

  • National Private Advisor to the President of Renamo for Political Affairs;
  • National Representative of Renamo;

2018: Elected Renamo’s Party Head list for the local elections in Maputo City and subsequently Director and Spokesperson of the Campaign, following his removal as head list by decision of the politically conditioned Electoral Administration Bodies;

2015 – 2018: Federal Deputy for MDM Party, assuming the role of Rapporteur of the Bench;

2016 – 2018:

  • Member of the National Political Commission of MDM Party;
  • Head of MDM Central Brigade for Maputo Province;

2014: Coordinator of the Electoral Office, in Maputo City, of MDM Candidate for the 2014 Presidential elections.

2014 – 2015: Head of the MDM Bench in Maputo City Municipal Assembly;

2013: Candidate for President of the Maputo City Council for MDM, having for the first time in history led the opposition to obtain 42% of the positions.
